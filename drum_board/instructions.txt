JLCPCB Ordering Instructions
1. Please visit www.jlcpcb.com and select "Add gerber file"
2. Select "gerbers.zip" in the file explorer popup, then press "Instant Quote".
3. Leave all ordering options as their default values, except "PCB Color". For reference, the ordering options are contained in "options.png". Options that must be changed are circled in red.
4. Please choose a shipping option other than "Global Standard Direct Line" unless unable to do otherwise.
